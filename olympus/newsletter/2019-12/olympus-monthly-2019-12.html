Done in November ✅

We significantly improved the form lead conversion rate of the buyer portal

After launching Avito Immobilier Neuf with the team in Casablanca in April this year, we were busy delivering some must-have components in the following 2 quarters, such as Search, Homepage, tools to support marketing, Client Portal and much more.


In Q4, we refocused our efforts on improving the performance of the buyer portal to drive more form leads to better satisfy our customers. The following chart shows the form lead conversion rate of the portal for organic traffic sources.

To find out how we did this, read on ...

We updated the project detail page

Firstly we addressed some problems with our mobile experience, such as there not being enough key information above the fold, "construction status" & "completion dates" not being clear enough, and that users were expecting the cover image to be “swipeable”.


However, we also removed the sticky contact bar at the bottom of the screen to make more space, but also to replace it with an inline "Contact" button, which drastically increased clicks to the form, which increased form leads.


Impact

  • +20.5% Form Lead CVR on project page
  • +8.9% increase in photo views

We improved our listing card design in Search & Homepage

We fixed problems such as “empty-looking” cards and prices not being easy to spot, while improving CTR with use of primary colour in Project Names. We also enabled a two-column list format on the mobile homepage, which helped drive more users from the homepage to project pages.


Impact

  • +16.7% Featured Project CTR
    (Overall funnel from homepage to project page +4.95%)
  • +4.6% increase in Search CTR

We added IP location targeting for Homepage Featured Projects

We introduced IP location targeting for the Featured Projects module on the homepage so that buyers are shown projects near to them.


Impact

  • +25.3% Featured Project CTR
    +56.1% for non-Casablanca users (since there was almost a 50% chance of Casablanca content already appearing due to concentration of supply there)

We drastically increased form leads outside office hours!

We discovered that 63% of Call Clicks were happening outside of normal Monday-to-Friday working hours, when builders would be less available to take calls.

With no call tracking solutions available in Morocco, we saw an opportunity to provide a different experience to buyers when builders’ offices are closed to encourage buyers to leave their contact details for a call-back - thus giving builders more high value form leads.


To test the hypothesis, we went simple and bold (!) by removing all phone numbers from ad views outside of office hours. The results were astounding...


Impact

  • +78% Form Lead CVR (!!) on the detail pages.
    (assuming the seller had a phone number)

We experimented with contact form fields

We experimented with removing non-essential contact form fields to boost conversion rate. We’re still reviewing the results but the indications are that we’re not seeing any positive impact with this change. Well… you can’t win them all! 😆

Coming in December 🔜

December is a short month but there’s still lots of value we want to create if we can. Here are some of the main items we plan to deliver.

Integrate Facebook Lead Ads with Olympus Leads Service

We’ll be supporting Avito’s efforts to drive more form leads for their new construction customers (even if they don’t happen on the Olympus portal) by integrating Facebook Lead Ads into the Olympus leads service so that they can be reported in the Olympus Client Portal.

Test search ranking to support Avito 5Leads30

"5Leads30" is set of Avito initiatives designed to achieve an aggressive goal to generate 5 form leads per day to all new projects for the first 30 days. Many of our current initiatives are focused on this goal. We see an opportunity in search to focus traffic on projects that need extra help in getting to this target.

Test contact CTAs & Forms in Search

Fotocasa generate 20% of their leads from their listings page. We’ll run an experiment to understand if a similar implementation will drive additional form leads or just redistribute/cannibalise those already occurring on the detail pages.

Homepage module experimentation

We’ll continue to experiment with new homepage listings modules and module ordering to improve engagement from this key entry point.
