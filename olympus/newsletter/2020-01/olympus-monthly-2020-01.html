We enabled Olympus leads to be generated outside of the Olympus Buyer Portal

Before this release, form leads could only be generated on the Olympus Buyer Portal. In December we unlocked the ability for the Avito team to use Facebook Lead Ads to generate leads without leaving Facebook while providing results to clients via the Olympus Client Portal (reports and leads access).


Compared to existing marketing on Facebook (which drives traffic to the portal), Avito sees a much lower average Cost Per Lead when using Facebook Lead Ads due to the reduced number of steps a buyer needs to go through.


The success of this initiative is very much down to the Avito team who are actively driving this. The Olympus involvement has primarly been to unlock this as a new source of leads and we look forward to seeing them grow it this year.


Impact

  • Total reported form leads increased by +46% in Dec vs Nov (despite only being live for 2.5 weeks) and have helped Avito ecplipse prior run rates.
  • New leads are generated at -78% lower CPL of existing traffic generation marketing efforts.

We tested Contact CTAs & Forms in Search

We knew that Fotocasa generated 20% of their leads from their listings page. But we wanted to understand if this would generate incremental leads or just redistribute them across multiple pages.


Overall the test was positive. We saw a higher overall form lead conversion rate and the overall form leads per user increased as users who converted on the listings page were more likely to conduct form leads on other projects.


Impact

  • +15.5% overall form lead conversion rate for users who visited the search page
  • +31.6% increase in "form leads per user" for users who already converted once

On 3 Jan, this feature was deployed to 100% of users.
